Tacos Pasta Shells

Ingrediant

4 Chicken Breasts
8 Slices of Emmental
8 slices of Ham
2 tbsp butter
2 tbsp Flour
1 tbsp Djion Musterd
6 tbsp Parmesan
Salt
Pepper

Tacos Pasta Shells

Step 1
Slice the chicken breast into 1 thin piece

Step 2
Cover with foil while flattening to provide an even level

Step 3
On each piece of chicken, place 2 slices of ham, 2 slices of Emmental cheese, and roll them up

Step 4
Put 2 tbsp butter on a preheated pan at 110°F

Step 5
Add 2 tbsp flour, 2 cups of milk, 1 tbsp Dijon mustard, 3 tbsp Parmesan, and a pinch of salt & pepper to the pan

Step 6
Pour the mixture over the chicken rolls

Step 7
Sprinkle a mixture of 1/2 cup breadcrumbs, 3 tbsp Parmesan, 1/4 cup parsley, and a pinch of salt over the top

Heat oven to 350°F. Cook and drain pasta shells as directed on box
